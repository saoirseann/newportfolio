University Work

The brief- To create

Design History Poster

The brief: create a Design History Poster based on a category out of 6 suggestions. I choose Pioneers of Digital Design and used the designer and artist April Greiman as inspiration. I like Greiman’s style through the fact that she incorporates vibrant colours and a unique style within all of her artwork and her typography




The Original Design

After receiving initial feedback, I had very few changes to make to my final submission. My lecturer advised that having the text “Exhibition” displayed within the poster was unnecessary. As you could already grasp what the sign is about through the venue and date shown on the bottom of my poster.
