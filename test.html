Hit the Switch

An out-going, innovative and creative designer from Belfast, Northern Ireland.

A User Experience and User Interface Designer with a passion for creating Human Centered Experiences that can impact everyday life. Currently studying Interaction Design at Belfast School of Art, I am constantly exploring new techniques to expand my design skills and knowledge.

I have worked within a creative team at Paperjam Design, producing responsive websites and digital experiences for client’s needs. Previously I have also worked in the Start-up environment with Aubergine, working in a small team to build and create a digital product for the health and well-being market.

Throughout my experience within the design industry I have learnt and applied many different methods that are key for a successful user experience such as accessibility, user research, user testing, benchmarking and more.

Have a project in mind? Check out my resume or drop me a line at hello@craig-allen.co.uk, I’m always interested in meeting new people and creating new designs.
